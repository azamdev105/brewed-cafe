Our Story

More Than Just Coffee.

Brewed was born in 2018 from a simple idea — that a great cup of coffee should come with a place worth sitting in. We source our beans directly from small farms, roast them in-house, and serve every cup with the same care we'd want for ourselves.

Whether you're here to work, catch up with a friend, or just breathe — this is your place.
